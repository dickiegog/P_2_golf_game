Welcome to our 9 Hole Golf course!

This is a golf game where you aim to complete each hole in as few swings as possible. The player with the lowest score wins!

Instructions:

  • Click "Swing" to take a shot.
  • Roll the dice to determine the outcome of your shot.
  • Try to get the ball into the hole in as few swings as possible.
  • If playing against the computer, select "Auto" to let the computer take its turn.
  • Click "New Game" to start a new game.
  • Click "Quit Game" to finish the game and see the final scores.
